I’m a software designer from the Netherlands. Currently, I work at Linear, helping companies build better software.

Memorable products solve real problems in an elegant way by striking the perfect balance between the user, product, and business. Although it can be a complex puzzle, it’s a rewarding process. I thrive on close collaboration with engineers and believe in the power of short iteration cycles. 

Over the past decade, I’ve worked solo, in small startups, and within larger organizations. I have worked as a freelancer, a full-time employee, and — more recently — as a founder.
